★ Why this home is exceptional
Priced correctly, 5824 Rudolph is one of the most valuable properties on Anastasia Island right now — and the market is moving away from it every month it sits.
This is a rare 5-bedroom, 3.5-bath oceanside home with panoramic Atlantic and Intracoastal views from all three floors, on a quiet dead-end street, one block to the surf, with no HOA and no CDD. It was meticulously remodeled in 2019 with no expense spared. The anchor comp — 5730 Rudolph, same street — went pending in just 15 days at $742/sqft, and is 700 sqft smaller than the subject. The blended 6-comp model lands the subject's fair value at ~$1.60M, while the anchor $/sqft alone signals closer to $2.0M — meaning the pricing strategy carries real upside if you execute the aggressive / bidding-war play. Since the 2021 purchase at $1.028M, the micro-market has run at an implied ~9.3% CAGR, and the appreciation curve continues to outrun the price the home would attract on a slow, aspirational list. The right move is to price for velocity, not patience.

How to read this

1. Comparable sales above are six recent Paragon MLS records from the same Anastasia Island Area 07 micro-market. Each one has been time-adjusted forward to today using a 9% / yr appreciation rate — calibrated to the subject property's actual 9.3% CAGR since its 2021 purchase.

2. The blended average $/sqft is multiplied by the subject's 2,720 sqft to produce a single fair-value number — that's the Implied Value shown in the Subject Property panel and at the top of the Pricing Strategy section.

3. The Pricing Strategy panel uses one slider that runs from −15% (loss-leader) to +35% (golden price). Move it and the recommended list price, expected days on market, expected offer count, likely close price, and the strategic narrative all update in real time. The panel's color and headline reflect which "zone" you're in:

• −15% to −10% · Loss-Leader — fastest sale, but you give up real money
• −10% to −5% · Aggressive — bidding-war play, recommended starting position
• −5% to +3% · At Market — clean sale, no drama, no upside
• +3% to +10% · Market Plus — slightly above the comp band, normal cycle
• +10% to +22% · Aspirational — patient list for the right cash buyer
• +22% to +35% · Golden Price — lottery ticket, long DOM, not recommended

4. Anchor signal vs blended model. The blended 6-comp model lands at ~$1.60M today. The single best one-to-one comparable — 5730 Rudolph (same street, same Butlers Beach pocket, pending in 15 days) — implies closer to $2.0M when you scale its $742/sqft up to the subject's 2,720 sqft. Wherever you set the slider, that $2.0M anchor signal is the upper guardrail to keep in mind: it's the reason the aggressive play is genuinely aggressive, not foolish.
